Bill Gates

Bill Gates (born October 28, 1955) is an American businessman, programmer, inventor, and philanthropist. He is the co-founder of Microsoft, one of the world's largest software companies.

Bill Gates comes from a wealthy family that has been prominent in society for three generations.

Attending the best school in Seattle and having the opportunity to develop programming skills from a young age allowed him to master the basics of the profession early on.

Bill enrolled at Harvard but dropped out after two years to fully dedicate himself to programming, organizing Microsoft with friends.

In 1994, Bill Gates married Melinda, whom he met at one of the company's briefings.
